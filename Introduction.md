# VL Costa Rica - AiDE Adoption Program

* **Context:** In Value Lab's Costa Rica office there has been a low conversion and retention rate of both engineers and administrative staff on Imagine's AiDE Platform.
* **Goal:** Develop an educational program for new adopters of AiDE.
* **Strategy:** 
	* Develop a knowledge repository made by users for users catered towards members of Costa Rica's office, in help induct them into Image's AiDE Platform through the creation of.
		* Written interactive articles similar to what you would encounter in developer documentation web sites.
		* Video guides.
	* Make the educational material easy to access for students.
* **Success Metrics:**
	* Office wide impact of AiDE is increased to at least 20% once the program goes live and into production on the first quarter.
	* AiDE's ACE Certification program success rate increases to at least 70%.

> [!NOTE]
> Usage is measured based on Value Lab's internal metrics, which is theorized as the total sum of `actual efforts`. The goal of 20 % is based on VL's last internal goal for the last performance review where this percentage was established company wide.
